For some farmers, Cabrio Duo is the best fungicide for beans farming. Pyraclostrobin is the main active ingredient of Cabrio Duo fungicide. It is a systemic fungicide with broad action on a lot of fungal diseases.

Cabrio duo fungicide can be used for almost all types of crops. It is registered to be used for addressing fungal issues on over 70 crops. These crops includes beans, vegetables, tree crops, fruit crops, cash crops, oil crops, forage crops and broad acre crops etc

Ridomil Gold Fungicide

Ridomil Gold fungicide is also one of the most effective fungicides for beans farming in Nigeria. Ridomil Gold fungicide has active ingredients of Metalaxyl-M 40g/Kg and Mancozeb 640g/Kg.

Ridomil Gold can be used to prevent and cure fungal diseases of beans like rust, souther blight, root rot, fusarium wilt and powdery mildew etc

Ridomil Gold fungicide comes as wettable granules. It is usually dissolved in water and sprayed to plants.

Five Star Fungicide

Five Star fungicide is one of the best fungicides for beans farming in Nigeria. It is a systemic fungicide with active ingredients of Azoxystrobin and Difenoconazole

Five star fungicide has preventive and curative actions on fungal diseases like beans rust disease, powdery mildew, southern blight and downy mildew etc.

Red Force Fungicide

Red Force fungicide is a contact and systemic fungicide that can be used to prevent and cure fungal diseases in beans crop. This fungicide is very effective as it has been tagged as the best fungicide for beans rust fungal disease by most beans farmers in Nigeria.

The Red Force fungicide has active ingredients of Metalaxyl and Copper Oxide. This fungicide can also be used to treat bacterial infection.

Glory Fungicide

The active ingredients of Glory fungicide are Mancozeb and Azoxystrobin. Glory fungicide is a contact and systemic fungicide. It is one of the best fungicides that can be used for beans farming in Nigeria.

This fungicide is very good for the prevention of beans rust disease and other fungal diseases that affects beans.

SAAF Fungicide

This fungicide can be used to prevent fungal diseases like powdery mildew, rust, southern blight and root rot etc.

Zebshi Fungicide

This fungicide is also one of the most effective fungicides for beans. Zebshi fungicide has an active ingredient of mancozeb. It is a contact fungicide and it is mostly used for prevention purpose.
